About the Developer

My name is Andrea Godsey, and I am twenty years old. I currently live in Springville, Indiana, with my family and two dogs. I enjoy reading mystery novels, such as Sherlock Holmes and Hercule Poirot in my spare time. I also enjoy creating drawings, and am currently working on a graphic novel series. I play video games as well, and have enjoyed watching cartoons and movies ever since I was a kid.

When I was around ten years old, I had been inspired by many of my cartoons I watched to become an animator. I loved the bright colors of all the characters, and enjoyed watching the story progress in each episode. I collect art books of famous movies and video games for this reason: to continue to find work that motivates me to continue towards my dream. Of course, I also wanted to have many talents in the technological field, such as web development. I wanted to have more than one talent, so I would be able to excel in my dream career. After all, there is no such thing as having too many talents.

I have created websites for about a year now, and am able to express my creativity in each page. I like having bright colors be the theme of my many websites. The theme, to me, has always been the most important aspect of a website. I excel in this area in particular, since I have always been able to come up with more ideas in a short period of time. This theme that appears in each of my websites, is what makes me stand out as a web developer.
