Sweet.io

I worked with the creators of Sweet.io to create a website that was easy for their customers to navigate and understand. This website is a one paged website, however there are links at the top of the page to take customers to the separate sections of the website. The home page lists all of the pages in one area. The about page contains two pictures of the creators, and describes the company as a whole. The contact page makes it easy for customers to contact the company about any questions or placing an order for catering. The products page consisted of pictures of the desserts, as well as prices and client testimonials about the products. There is a white space between each page as well.
