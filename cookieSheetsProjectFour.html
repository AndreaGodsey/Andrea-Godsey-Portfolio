Cookie Sheets

In a collaborative effort with two other students, we created a counterfeit bakery website that specializes in the selling of cookies. For the project, we researched the area to identify the most popular business type. After reaching the conclusion to create a bakery website, I was chosen to become the group leader. Throughout the project, I designed the interface, came up with the design aesthetic, tracked important dates, assigned task to my group members, and presented our finished project to the class. The website had an order page, menu, a login page, shopping cart, about, and a nutrition table for allergies. This website was developed in the PHP Storm web development program. This coding platform is known to be most common in professional websites around the globe. The results of our website were highly effective and successful. Regarding my experience, this project shows how well I can lead a team and still create a masterfully crafted website through group effort.
