The Soap Factory

I worked with the creators of the Soap Factory to create a useable website for customers to order their products easily. There are four pages to this website, listed as Home, About, Products, and Contact information. The home page consists of a small summary about what the company has to offer. The about page consists of information about the creators of the company, and its creation. The contacts page makes it easy for customers to ask questions or to make requests to the company. Finally, there is their products page, consisting of pictures and descriptions of their soap. There is an image at the top of the page that is consistent throughout the website, as well as sideways text to tell about what page the viewer is on.
